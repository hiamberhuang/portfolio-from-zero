# Inspiration — finding your visual reference system｜找你的视觉参考系

You cannot brief an AI to build something beautiful if you can't point at what "beautiful" means to you. This stage is about collecting references until **your own taste becomes visible to you.**

> 你没法让 AI 做出"好看"的东西，如果你自己都指不出"好看"是什么样。这一步是收集参考，直到**你自己的审美对你自己可见**。

## The collection workflow｜收集流程

1. Open a simple table (Feishu Base, Notion, Figma board). · 开一张简单的表（飞书多维表 / Notion / Figma 板）
2. Screenshot 10–15 sites you have a genuine reaction to. · 截 10–15 个你真有反应的站
3. Tag each with 1–3 words: `minimalist` / `editorial` / `playful` / `3D` / `warm`. · 每个打 1–3 个 tag
4. After ~15, the tags cluster. **That cluster is your taste.** · 收够 ~15 个，tag 自然聚类——**那个聚类就是你的审美**

---

## The output you need｜你需要的产出

Translate the cluster into **3–4 nameable design tokens**:

> 把聚类翻译成 **3–4 个能说出口的设计 token**：

- **Vibe 调性**: e.g. "editorial, warm, a little playful" · "编辑感、温暖、一点俏皮"
- **Color 色彩**: e.g. "cream background, one deep accent" · "米白底 + 一个深色强调"
- **Type 字体**: e.g. "elegant serif headlines + clean sans body" · "优雅衬线标题 + 干净无衬线正文"
- **Density 密度**: e.g. "generous whitespace, large type" · "大留白、大字号"

These four lines are what you hand the AI in Stage 6. Specific tokens → specific output. Vague taste → corporate boilerplate.

> 这四行就是你 Step 6 喂给 AI 的东西。token 具体 → 产出具体；审美模糊 → 给你套模板。

---

## Storytelling beats technology｜叙事胜过技术

The best-aging portfolios are not the most technically impressive — they're the ones with the clearest story. A narrative-strong site built in a pre-AI era still gets "this is amazing" reactions today. Collect for **story and clarity.**

> 最耐看的作品集不是技术最炫的，是故事最清楚的。一个非 AI 时代做的强叙事网站，今天还能收到"绝了"的反应。收集时盯着**故事和清晰度**，别只盯特效。
