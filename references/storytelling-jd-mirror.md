# The JD-Mirror Method — the core of this skill｜JD 镜像法（本 skill 的核心）

Most people get stuck here. They can find visual references (everyone knows to do that), but they freeze on **what to actually say about themselves.** This is the method that unblocks it.

> 大多数人卡在这一步。找视觉参考谁都会，但一到**该说自己什么**就卡住。这就是破局的方法。

---

## Why storytelling is the real leverage｜为什么叙事才是真杠杆

A portfolio is, literally, a portfolio — a vehicle for someone to decide about you fast. The visitor's brain runs three questions in the first 30 seconds:

> 作品集本质就是 portfolio——让对方快速对你下判断的载体。访客打开后 30 秒内脑子里跑三个问题：

- **Who are you?** · 你是谁？
- **What have you done?** · 你做过什么？
- **Where is your value?** · 你的价值在哪？

If the site doesn't answer those three, no amount of animation rescues it. Technology is never the bottleneck. Story is.

> 网站不回答这三个，再多动效也救不了。技术从来不是瓶颈，故事才是。

---

## The method: mirror the job description｜方法：镜像目标岗位的 JD

The fastest way to find your storyline is to **reverse-engineer it from the role you most want.**

> 找故事线最快的方法：**用你最想去的岗位反向给自己写题。**

1. **Find the JD of the single role you want most.** The excitement matters — it means the role fits.
   > **找你最想去的那一个岗位的 JD。** 看到就兴奋很重要——说明这个岗位 fit。
2. **Extract its 4–6 core required capabilities.**
   > **抽出它要的 4–6 个核心能力。**
3. **Make each capability a section, answered with your own evidence.**
   > **每个能力对应网站一个 section，用你自己的证据回应。**

---

## Worked example｜真实例子

A real AI-marketing JD asked for, in essence: *someone who deeply understands brand social marketing AND can use AI thinking to amplify growth — not a pure consultant, not a pure executor, but the client's "growth brain."*

> 一个真实的 AI 营销岗 JD 核心要的是：*既懂品牌社媒营销、又能用 AI 思维放大增长的人——不是纯咨询、不是纯执行，而是客户的"增长外脑"。*

| JD capability JD 能力 | Site section 网站 section |
|---|---|
| Full-funnel brand social marketing 品牌社媒全案 | Three real platform cases (hard numbers) · 3 个平台真实 case + 硬数据 |
| Client communication & delivery 客户沟通与交付 | Speaking photos + brand wall · 演讲照片 + 品牌墙 |
| Openness to AI / learning AI 开放度与学习力 | An "AI Toolkit" section with self-built skills · AI Toolkit + 自写 skills |
| Deep thinking & retrospection 深度思考与复盘 | Each case ends with "How I'd rebuild this in 2026 with AI" · 每个 case 收一段"2026 我会怎么用 AI 重做" |

Every section is a direct response to something the hiring manager already told you they care about. When they open the site, it feels built for them — because it was.

> 每个 section 都是对招聘方亲口说在乎的东西的直接回应。他们打开网站会觉得"这是为我做的"——因为确实是。

---

## The universal skeleton｜万能骨架

90% of personal sites resolve to five sections. Start here, then let the JD-Mirror tell you what to emphasize:

> 90% 的个人站逃不开这五个 section。从这开始，再让 JD 镜像告诉你重点放哪：

**Hero · About · Experience · Cases · Contact**

- **Hero** — your one-line thesis; the 30-second hook · 一句话定位，30 秒钩子
- **About** — the human; identity and POV · 人本身，身份与观点
- **Experience** — trajectory · 轨迹
- **Cases** — proof, with real numbers + what you'd do differently now · 证据，真实数字 + 现在会怎么重做
- **Contact** — make the next step frictionless · 让下一步零摩擦

Add or rename sections only when the JD-Mirror demands it (e.g. an "AI Toolkit" section because the role values AI fluency).

> 只有 JD 镜像要求时才加 / 改 section（比如岗位看重 AI，就加个 AI Toolkit）。

---

## If you don't have direct experience｜如果你没有直接经验

Career-switchers worry the JD asks for things they've never done. Two moves:

> 转行的人担心 JD 要的自己没做过。两招：

1. **Translate adjacent experience.** "Led content growth" maps onto "demand generation." Find the real overlap; don't fabricate.
   > **翻译相邻经验。** "带内容增长"可以映射到"需求生成"。找真实重叠，别编。
2. **Show the learning curve as evidence.** A self-built project, an open-source skill, a documented experiment prove "learns fast and ships" — often the capability a switcher is weighted on most.
   > **把学习曲线本身当证据。** 一个自建项目、一个开源 skill、一段记录在案的实验，证明"学得快、能交付"——这往往是转行者被最看重的能力。

Never claim a capability you can't back. The site surfaces real overlap, it doesn't manufacture it.

> 永远别 claim 你撑不起来的能力。网站是把真实重叠浮出来，不是凭空造。

---

## The starter prompt｜起手 prompt

Hand the AI your résumé + your dream-role JD, and let it propose the section structure. Copy-paste:

> 把你的简历 + 理想岗位 JD 丢给 AI，让它先提议 section 结构。直接复制：

```
这是我想参考的网站：https://amberhuang.world/
附件是我的简历 & 我的理想岗位描述。
请结合我的理想岗位描述帮我做一个作品集网站，告诉我：
作品集应该包含哪些部分、体现哪些能力，
以及有哪些部分加上会加分、但我目前没提供给你的——可以主动跟我要。

(EN) Here's a site I want to reference: https://amberhuang.world/
Attached: my résumé + my dream-role JD.
Based on the JD, design my portfolio: which sections it should have,
which capabilities each should prove, and what would strengthen it
that I haven't given you yet — ask me for it.
```

## Prep checklist before you start｜动手前准备清单

- [ ] Résumé 简历
- [ ] The target JD 目标岗位 JD
- [ ] Past project material (if any) 过往项目资料（若有）
- [ ] Photos: LinkedIn headshot, life shots, workplace shots 领英照 / 生活照 / 职场照

## Output of this stage｜本阶段产出

A section-by-section content outline, in your own words, each section tied to a JD capability. This is exactly what you feed the AI in the build stage — content first, then design.

> 一份逐 section 的内容大纲，用你自己的话写，每个 section 对应一条 JD 能力。这正是 build 阶段喂给 AI 的东西——先内容，再设计。
